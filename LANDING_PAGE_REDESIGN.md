### **Framer Motion Animations:**

1. **Entrance Animations:**
   - `initial={{ y: 50, opacity: 0 }}`
   - `whileInView={{ y: 0, opacity: 1 }}`
   - Smooth fade-up on scroll

2. **Hover Effects:**
   - `whileHover={{ scale: 1.05, y: -5 }}`
   - Lift and scale on hover
